BARGAIN hunters are racing to get to their nearest Card Factory, desperate to get their hands on the new-in festive finds.

So if you’re a Harry Potter fan, or know someone who is, then you’ve come to the right place.

With Christmas fast approaching, shoppers are on the hunt for the best gifts, but with many of our purses feeling tighter than ever before, if we can find a nice gift for a bargain, it’s a win win. 

One savvy shopper was left stunned when browsing her local Card Factory, when she spotted a large range of Harry Potter items.

Gem Louise Angell took to social media to show off her cheap finds.

Posting on Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, a private Facebook group with a staggering 2.3 million members, Gem shared pictures of the stocking fillers and wrote: “Found these at Card Factory."

She then added: "£7.99 for the hot chocolate train is a bargain.”

Gem spotted a Hogwarts Express hot chocolate gift set, with four different colour changing hot chocolate mixes and marshmallows, which was just £7.99.

Not only this, but she also found a sugar cookie baking set, for just £3.99 and a hot drink bomb set, also for £3.99. 

Gem also managed to get her hands on a Hermione model paint set, which includes six paints and a paintbrush, for £3.99.

As well as this, there’s also a 250 piece Dobby jigsaw, which comes in a Hogwarts sock, for just £3.99.

But if that wasn’t enough, Card Factory are also selling a self-stirring mug, for £4.99, and a colourful cup and straw, also £4.99.

If you’re looking to buy a gift for someone with a sweet tooth, there’s a chocolate flavour cake mix with sugar strands set, which only takes two minutes to make and comes in a Hogwarts mug, for just £6.99.

Gem shared her post just last week, but it has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly amassed 782 likes and 1,000 comments. 

Social media users were very impressed with the Harry Potter range and many took to the comments to express this. 

One person said: “Think I need to take a trip.” 

Another added: “Good little stocking fillers.”

A third commented: “The hot chocs look wicked.” 

Meanwhile, someone else wrote: “I need the self stirring mug.”
