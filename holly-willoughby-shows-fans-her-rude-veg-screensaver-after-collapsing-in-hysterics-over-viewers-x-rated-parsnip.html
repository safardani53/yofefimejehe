THIS Morning's Holly Willoughby showed fans her "rude veg" screensaver - after collapsing in hysterics over a viewer's X-rated parsnip.

The 39-year-old host shared a hilarious clip this morning joking that that she had a new steamy screensaver.

The mum-of-two posted an Instagram Story featuring the naughty looking parsnip alongside a carrot that urgently needed a "wax".

Holly and Phillip Schofield were left stunned yesterday by a series of cheeky images that were sent in by viewers.

But two photos left the pair in stiches after it resembled a specific body part.

The duo were shocked by the the cheeky image sent in by viewer Nadine.

"Wow. I can't even look at it," screeched Holly.

But it wasn't long before she was mesmerised by the oddly-shaped vegetable - and even vowed to make it her screensaver.

"That's amazing. That's going to be my new screensaver. It's so good."

Holly and her co-host Phillip Schofield threw their heads back laughing over the naughty snaps.

They were sent hundreds by cheeky viewers who brightened up the morning.

The presenters had asked to see pictures of fans' food that looked oddly familiar.

They ranged from homegrown potatoes that looked like a hamster and a phallic ginger.

Another potato looked like Lord Voldemort from the Harry Potter films.
