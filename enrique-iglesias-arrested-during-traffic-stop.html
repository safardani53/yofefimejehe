Enrique Iglesias: ARRESTED During Traffic Stop

 Posted on May 15, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  174 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

May was a rough month for sexy singer Enrique Iglesias. First, Enrique sliced his hand on a drone, badly hurting himself.

Now, reports are surfacing that also in May, Enrique was arrested in Miami!

While pulled over, Enrique tried to slip out of the driver’s seat.  This not-so-slick move landed him in handcuffs.

According to Miami police, Iglesias was behind the wheel of a white Cadillac Escalade when cops stopped him for driving in a closed express lane.  If you are driving with a suspended license, why are you breaking driving laws???  Quick way to get busted…again.

The cops said that the singer tried to jump into the back seat while the passenger, his tour manager, attempted to get in the driver’s seat.

Apparently, Enrique has a suspended license, which prompted the switcheroo. Did they really think this was going to work???

Enrique and his manager were arrested, and both have pled not guilty to obstruction.  Iglesias has also pled not guilty to driving with a suspended license.
