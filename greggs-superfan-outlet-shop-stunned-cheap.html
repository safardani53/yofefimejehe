A GREGGS superfan took a trip to an outlet shop and was left stunned at how cheap everything was.

Cory, who posts on TikTok under the username @corysworldd, is a food fan from Wales. 

The 26-year-old Welsh man regularly posts ‘What I eat in a day’ videos to the video sharing platform.

He has amassed an impressive 177.1k followers and 9.4million likes on the social media app.

In one of his recent clips, Cory, who many know as ‘Greggs on legs’, took a trip to a Greggs Outlet store in Barry, a town in Wales.

The Greggs Outlet stores, which are in England, Scotland and Wales, sell day-old goods for half price and less.

Cory spotted cheap sandwiches, pasties and sweet treats, many of which were under £1 each, with some on buy one get one free. 

He said: “Come to second hand Greggs with me.
