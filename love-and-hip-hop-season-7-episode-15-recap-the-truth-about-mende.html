Love &amp; Hip Hop Season 7 Episode 15 Recap: The Truth About Mendeecees

 Posted on September 30, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  775 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Did everyone manage to put their beef aside on the reunion special of VH1’s hit reality series?

That was their plan on Love & Hip Hop Season 7 Episode 15 when Nina Parker rounded all of the cast members up to run down the crazy events. 

When the episode got underway, Nina asked Cardi B about what was going on with Asia. Cardi had a long and hilarious explanation about the whole thing.

"She is just ma because I have a big butt and he wants that. I have beef in the hood, but I know why I have beef. I have no idea why this girl is mad at me.”

Pretty much instantly, Asia was brought out from backstage and asked about her issues with Cardi. 

“I didn’t have a problem with Cardi.” Cardi jumps up and butts in. She asks Asia “Why were you talking mad trash about me in those text messages?”

Asia says “I supported Swift when he started working with you until you started acting funny.” All of a sudden Cardi gets up to throws her fist and hits Asia in the head.

Nina says “Everybody in their seats and let’s talk like adults.” Really, Nina? There are were no adults present in the studio. 

Nina tried again to get to the root of the issue. “What was it that caused you to send those text messages to Swift?”

Asia says “Swift disrespected me.” Cardi was not impressed, saying “You didn’t like me before all of that. Why didn’t you like me?”

Asia says “I went through Swift’s Facebook and I found you. I stalked you.”

Swift appeared and apologized to Asia, before confirming he was not currently in a relationship with either woman. 

Nina switched gears to all the drama with the Creep Squad. 

Nina asks Cisco “What is the problem with you and the other guys?”

Cisco says “I don’t know what Rich’s problem is. He seems to have issues with me. I have tried to get Peter to sit down and resolve it.”

Peter says “I shouldn’t have to keep sitting down with two grown men and trying to resolve issues.”

Nina then asked to know the origins of the group. This resulted in Rich and Cisco having to be separated by security. 

Nina decided to chat to Mariahlynn because she felt that she would be able to shed some light on what’s been going on with the guys. 

Mariahlynn says “Cisco felt betrayed by the situation, so he said some hurtful things about me which he apologized for. Our mother’s both have brain aneurysms, so we connected on that level.”

Cisco apologized to Mariahlynn about the way he acted. He then gave Peter a check for $10,000 to make up for the money he took from the appearance in New Orleans. 

After that, we got an update on Snoop, and J. Nina asked J about the Sofie Green audition drama. J’s reply was pretty tame as opposed to some of her drama from earlier in the season.

“I just felt disrespected. All of this foolishness was just ridiculous.”

Snoop cuts in and asks J “Did you cheat on me?” This caused yet another huge argument with the ladies throwing accusations at each other. 

Snoop confirmed there was no way she could get back together with J. J burst into floods of tears as she said how much she loved Snoop.

“I love her. That doesn’t just go away, but I feel like I deserve respect and all of this was just messy.” Snoop then says “I will always love J as a friend and be there for her.”  

When it came to trying to establish what went wrong with all the women in Mendeecees’ life, Yandy was quick to point out that she did not give a damn about the women before her. 

Erika got upset when a phone interview with Mendeecees confirmed that she was a side chick. 

She says, “As a man with a daughter, mother and sister I think it is very shameful of him to sit up here and talk like that.”

Yandy was quick to chime in. 

“That was always the story I got from him. If you know you have to leave his house by a certain time and can’t be around his friends and family, then that’s your position. You need to play your position."

The ladies then had to be locked in their dressing rooms to stop them from fighting.
