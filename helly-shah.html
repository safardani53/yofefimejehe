Helly Shah Biography

Helly Shah is a Gujarati model and actress. She was born in Ahmedabad on January 7, 1996. Helly was interested in acting since she was a youngster and began her profession in the eighth grade. She later made her television debut with the program Gulal. Helly will make her Bollywood debut in the forthcoming film Kaya Palat. Gautam Shah, his father, is a well-known businessman in Gujarat. Vanlata Shah is the name of her Mother. She also has a sibling, Priyam, who is now pursuing his degree.

She finished her primary and secondary education at M.K. Secondary and Higher Secondary School in Ahmedabad. She then enrolled in Gujarat Technological University in Ahmedabad, where she earned her Bachelor of Science degree.

Career

Shah started her acting career in the eighth school. Gulaal, a program on Star Plus, launched her career. She played Shruti in Diya Aur Baati Hum in 2011. She later appeared in Life OK’s Alaxmi – Hamari Super Bahu as Laxmi. She went on to portray Ami in Khelti Hai Zindagi Aankh Micheli. After that, she starred in Sony Pal’s Khushiyon Kii Gullak Aashi.

Shah played Swara Maheshwari in Swaragini from March 2015 until December 2016. Shah appeared in Colors TV’s Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa in 2016. (season 9). She got the Indian Television Academy Award for Best Actress Popular in 2017 for her role as Devanshi Upadhyay/Bakshi in Devanshi.

In 2019, she played the dual roles of Saltanat Shah and Kaynaat Shah alongside Rajveer Singh in Star Bharat’s Sufiyana Pyaar Mera, for which she received the Gold Award for Best Actress in Negative Role. She then played Neha in Star Plus’s Yeh Rishtey Hain Pyaar Ke.

Shah played Riddhima Vansh Raisinghania in Ishq Mein Marjawan 2 with Rrahul Sudhir and Vishal Vashishtha, which aired on Colors TV from 13 July 2020 to 13 March 2021. After the program ended, a new season titled Ishq Mein Marjawan 2: Naya Safar began streaming on Voot Select on March 15, with Shah repeating the role of Riddhima. On May 18, 2022, she made her Cannes red carpet debut

On May 21, she debuted the poster for her first film, Kaya Palat, at the Cannes Film Festival. The actress also walked the red carpet for L’Oréal Paris at the Cannes Film Festival, making her the first Indian television actress to do so.
