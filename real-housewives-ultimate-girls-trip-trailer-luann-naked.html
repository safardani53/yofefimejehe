LUANN de Lesseps stripped down and covered herself with aluminum foil before running down the beach in the trailer for the new Peacock original spin-off series.

The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip will be released on November 18th and will feature a whole slew of drama as cast members from different cities unite under one household.

Luann, 56, ran naked across the beach in the first look trailer, covered in sunscreen and aluminum foil.

The reality star used the shiny material to cover her body as she bolted completely nude on the boardwalk.

The TV personality's hair looked wild and unkempt as the foil began sliding off her greasy body, threatening to bare all.

Luann screamed: "I'm losing my foil!" as cameras nearly caught her naked figure.

Later in the trailer, Ramona Singer, 64, wore a clear mouthpiece that exposed all of her teeth and gums during a pajama party game.

The blonde shouted incomprehensible words through the mouth guard before turning and running towards the bathroom.

HOUSEWIVES REUNION

The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip trailer proved that the season will be full of drama, as women from the series across the country joined together under one household in Turks and Caicos.

The housewives participated in various games, drank copious amounts of alcohol, and had their fair share of arguments during the teaser clip for the new spin-off.

A Bravo insider previously revealed to The Sun that the official all-stars' lineup will include Jersey girl Teresa, 48, and New York socialite Ramona, 64.

Joining them will be fellow RHONY star Luann de Lesseps, and Teresa’s RHONJ castmate and sister-in-law, Melissa Gorga.

Real Housewives Of Atlanta stars Kenya Moore, 50, and Cynthia Bailey, 54, will also be present for the festivities.

Kyle Richards, 52, will be the only Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills member to be featured, representing the West Coast.

LUXURIOUS ACCOMMODATIONS

The Sun exclusively revealed in August that the ladies stayed in a $9k-a-night Turk's villa while they filmed the new series.

Occupying more than two acres, the stunning beachfront property spans over 10,000sq feet and is surrounded by luscious green trees.

The oceanside estate has seven bedrooms, eight bathrooms, two pools, a hot tub, private tennis and bocce ball courts, and a villa butler.

The housewives hardly needed to lift a finger during their stay, as the network provided a butler, housekeeper, pool person, security guard, personal concierge, and a chef who cooked up to three meals a day for the entire group. 

The Bravo stars shared plenty of photos while they basked in the tropical oasis, flaunting their bikini bodies on social media.

EXCLUSIVE CAST

Although the network had hoped they could include more Real Housewives All-stars on the spin-off, a production insider told The Sun that they were forced to limit the cast due to "Covid protocols."

"The cast size had to be cut down to seven, as the largest-sized house the show could book is seven bedrooms due to Covid protocols," they explained.
