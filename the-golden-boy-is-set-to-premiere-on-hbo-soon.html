The Golden Boy Is Set To Premiere On HBO Soon

 Posted on March 2, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  546 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The Golden Boy, a two-part HBO documentary, is planned to premiere on July 24, 2023, at 9 p.m. ET. It covers Oscar De La Hoya’s path as a retired professional boxer and promoter.

The Golden Boy’s official tagline is:

“THE GOLDEN BOY peels back the layers of this celebrated yet complicated figure, exploring his triumphs and turmoil to reveal a man struggling to come to terms with lifelong demons.”

Oscar De La Hoya, dubbed “The Golden Boy,” is well-known not just for his spectacular boxing career but also for his tremendous effect on the sport as a promoter. He created Golden Boy Promotions in 2002 after quitting professional boxing in 2009. Since then, the organization has grown to become one of the most important and powerful boxing promotion companies in the industry.

Oscar De La Hoya is the focus of The Golden Boy.

The trailer for this HBO documentary provides a small glimpse inside Oscar De La Hoya’s multifaceted life. According to the teaser, the documentary will dive into the nuances of De La Hoya’s life, showing both his accomplishments and his emotional troubles. It alludes to a personal journey of a man dealing with lifetime issues, providing audiences with a better insight into the difficulties he encountered behind the scenes. The trailer promises a thought-provoking investigation of De La Hoya’s life and gives a sense of the documentary’s emotional depth and contemplation.

The Golden Boy’s plot

This documentary chronicles Oscar De La Hoya, a youthful boxing prodigy who won Olympic gold and many world championships before the age of 19. De La Hoya’s narrative enthralled the Mexican-American community in East Los Angeles, and he was hailed as a neighborhood hero and role model. Behind the polished exterior and the moniker “The Golden Boy,” however, is a complicated and disturbed man.

The docuseries peels back the layers of De La Hoya’s life, exposing his accomplishments, inner turmoil, and lifelong problems via honest interviews with De La Hoya and those closest to him, as well as a rare archive video. It illustrates the incredible strain he was subjected to, as well as his battle to reconcile his public image with his personal troubles, shining light on the unknown truths behind the surface of a revered hero.

Who exactly is Oscar De La Hoya?

Oscar De La Hoya is a boxing promoter and former professional fighter. On February 4, 1973, he was born in Los Angeles, California, USA. De La Hoya had a remarkable boxing career in which he competed in many weight classes and won numerous titles, including an Olympic gold medal in 1992 and multiple world championships in various weight classes. He is well-known for his remarkable boxing abilities and captivating demeanor, and he is widely regarded as one of the most popular and marketable boxers of his generation. De La Hoya formed Golden Boy Promotions, a successful boxing promotion firm, after retiring from fighting, and he has continued to contribute to the sport.


Fernando Villena is the director of this documentary. Mark Wahlberg, Stephen Levinson, Archie Gips, Mario Lopez, Jeffrey Stearns, and David Wendell serve as executive producers. ViaMar Productions and Universal Television Alternative Studio (UTAS) collaborated on the production, demonstrating the joint efforts of outstanding professionals to bring this intriguing documentary to life.
