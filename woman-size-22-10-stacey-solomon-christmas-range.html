CHRISTMAS party season is just around the corner.

And ahead of the holidays, people are already hitting the shops to try and find the perfect outfits for their festive celebrations.

Iz and El frequently share TikTok videos of themselves trying on clothes to show how they look on their different bodies, and decided to give Stacey Solomon's newly-released Christmas collection for InTheStyle a try.

The pair took to their Straight to the Curve TikTok page to share the video, which began with them trying on a forest green ribbed loungewear set - complete with a pearl-encrusted top and flared trousers.

The top is currently on sale for £32, while the trousers are £27.

They then tried on a cute £36 black jumper dress, Iz teaming hers with black Converse and El opting for knee-high boots.

Upping the glamour, they then swapped the jumper dress for a gorgeous silver sparkly wrap number, which clung to their figures in all the right places.

They both teamed it with strappy sandals, showing that the dress - also £36 - is perfect for a Christmas party.

"All the cosy and christmas vibes with the new @In The Style @staceysolomon collection," the pair captioned the TikTok video.

"I love these!" one person commented on the video.
