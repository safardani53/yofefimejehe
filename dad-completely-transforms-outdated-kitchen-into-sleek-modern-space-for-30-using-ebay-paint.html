Dad completely transforms outdated kitchen into sleek modern space for 30 using eBay paint

 Posted on July 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  246 words  |  Aldo Pusey

A DAD has shared how he completely transformed his kitchen with just two tins of paint. 

Instead of needing to fork out thousands for a brand new cooking space, the savvy homeowner just spent £30 on Johnstones paint from eBay. 

He shared photos of his old, plain kitchen, which had dark brown counters and was in desperate need of some TLC. 

In the impressive after shot, the room is now grey and looks like a bright modern space. 

Taking to the Facebook group Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK, he said: “My kitchen project the paint cost £30 for 2 tins off ebay!! Used Johnstones cupboard paint.”

And unsurprisingly, it has seriously impressed social media users, who have said they are keen to replicate the look in their own homes. 

For those asking, he shared how he used the Johnstones cupboard paint in shade pale grey, which came with a primer mixed in. 

One person raved: “That is beautiful. Looks like you’ve spent thousands!”

Another added: “Well done think lockdown has made us all DIY experts lol.”

And one commented: “Good job looks great.”
