If the rumors are true, Robert Pattinson will have a lot to live up to as the new face of Dior.

A month after releasing Jennifer Lawrence‘s stunning first ads for Miss Dior, they’ve followed up with Marion Cotillard’s latest campaign images.

PHOTOS: See more stunning celebrity ad campaigns here!

The actress looks like a 1950s screen star in the shots, sporting sleek curled locks and a full-skirted evening gown (below) next to bold statement bags by the line.

It’s impossible for Cotillard to look bad in anything, but we have to say, this particular look (we’re calling it “Brunette Grace Kelly”) really suits her. What do you think of her latest ads? Tell us in the comments below!
