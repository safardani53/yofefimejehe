Netflix fans terrified Shadow and Bone will be axed after two seasons - despite show topping streame

 Posted on March 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  274 words  |  Larita Shotwell

NETFLIX viewers have begged the streaming service to not pull the plug on hit Shadow and Bone amid a plethora of cancelled shows on the site.

The show managed to work itself up to the top of Netflix's ranking but it hasn't stopped fans from fearing the worst for the show.

Currently, Netflix has aired two seasons of the fantasy show which is based upon the books of Leigh Bardugo.

The second series was released last week and made its way to the top of the most-watched list knocking off fan favourite You.

Netflix has been under the microscope after a series of shows were sensationally axed over the past few months leaving fans furious.

However, the campaign trail to give Shadow and Bone a third series has already begun.

On Twitter, one fan wrote: “Last episode was complete and total chaos they really threw every storyline in there but I’m soooo here for it.

“Plz plz @netflix renew @shadowandbone_ bc I NEED NEED more of this in my life.”

Another added: “I need season 3 of Shadow and Bone so bad they better renew it."

While a third fan demanded another season from Netflix bosses as they forcefully said: “Season 2 is INCREDIBLE AND I NEED @netflix to RENEW FOR SEASON THREE RIGHT THE F*** NOW."

The show revolves around Alina Starkov, an orphaned map-maker who realises that she has a series of special gifts.

Among the cast are Jessie Mei Li, Archie Renaux, Freddy Carter and British acting great Zoe Wanamaker as well as Skins legend Luke Pasqualino.

Shadow and Bone seasons 1 and 2 are available on Netflix.
