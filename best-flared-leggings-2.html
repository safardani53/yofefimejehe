ON the lookout for a new staple in your wardrobe? We've got your back (well, legs) with our roundup of the best flared leggings available to shop now.

Known once upon a time as yoga pants, this 90s stalwart is seriously back in vogue for 2022 and it's not just for workouts and lounging. Oversized tees, cropped jumpers and statement blazers are just some of the pieces you can team this garment with.

From the close-fit waist to the kick-flare that gives more freedom than classic silhouettes, there's something elevated about flared leggings that fashion lovers can't get enough of right now.

Whether you're off out to grab a coffee, chilling at home, or dressing up for a dinner date, comfortable yet polished flared leggings have your back.

The most popular style are those classic black high-waisted pairs, but there are lots of other colours and prints all over the high street that are just as snug.

Where to buy flared leggings

Flared leggings are massively on-trend right now, so you'll find them all over the high street and online. We spotted some great fashion styles from the likes of Mango, Urban Outfitters and H&M, and of course, Amazon has a huge array of options.

What to wear with flared leggings

This is a trend you can dress up or down, depending on where you're going. For WFH chic, team with an oversized white tee that you can throw a blazer over when you need to pop out.

On dressier days, flared leggings look great worn with a fitted long sleeve top and boots with a stacked heel. And for a polished everyday look, you can't go wrong with a roomy knitted jumper or longline blouse topped off with a leather jacket or tailored coat.

If you really want to embrace the retro style, go for a funky pair of printed leggings and a pair of white sneakers, which is perfect for the festival season.

For more inspo, why not explore the #flaredleggings and #flarepants hashtags on Instagram?

To help you navigate this loungewear must-have, we've found the best flared leggings to shop for now. Let's find your flare!
