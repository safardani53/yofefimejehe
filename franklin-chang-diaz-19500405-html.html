Franklin Ramón Chang Díaz is a mechanical engineer, physicist and former NASA astronaut. He is the sole founder and current CEO of the Rocket Company Ad Astra as well as a member of the Cummins Board of Directors. He became a US citizen in 1977. He is of Costa Rican and Chinese descent. He's a veteran of seven Space Shuttle missions, setting the record, from 2021, for most space flight. He is the third Latin American, but the first Latin American immigrant NASA astronaut to be selected into space. Chang Díaz is a member of the NASA Astronaut Hall of Fame.
