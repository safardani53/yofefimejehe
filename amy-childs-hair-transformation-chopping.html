Amy Childs shows off dramatic hair transformation after chopping long locks off

 Posted on July 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  339 words  |  Larita Shotwell

AMY Childs has debuted a new look complete saying 'bl***y loves it'.

Towie star Amy, 31, chopped off her auburn waves to unveil a chic, long bob hairstyle.

The mum of two showed off her new locks on her Instagram stories, putting a poll to her followers.

She wanted to know if they liked her look, with the options of replying 'yes Amy' or 'short hair don't care'.

Amy posed with her straight, shoulder-length hair and swished it for the camera as she clearly enjoyed her new style.

"You don't understand how long I have wanted to do this for," she captioned the Instagram story.

Her new hair comes as she gets serious with First Dates star Billy Delbosq after her split from ex Tim.

She has been growing close to the gym owner - despite saying he was "growing tired of Essex girls" while searching for love on the E4 show.

Amy has been gushing over her new beau on Insta, admitting she was in 'in love'.

Billy starred on First Dates back in 2018.

He caught Amy's attention months after her split from boyfriend Tim who she had called "The One".

The heartbroken TV star ended their year-long relationship in July.

Amy fled to Ibiza with her daughter Polly, four, who she shares with her ex Bradley Wright.

She had confirmed their romance with a shot of the two of them together in May 2020.

In a now-deleted snap, the Essex beauty wrote: "When you finally meet the right one for you, it suddenly becomes clear why everyone else was so wrong …. ❤️ I love you"

Underneath the heartfelt declaration, boyfriend Tim replied: “Love you”.

Amy lived with Tim during lockdown at her Brentwood home alongside daughter Polly and two-year-old son Ritchie Jnr, who she shares with businessman Ritchie.
