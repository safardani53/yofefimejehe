Who is Teen Mom 2 star Kailyn Lowry and how many children does she have?

 Posted on April 26, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  664 words  |  Aldo Pusey

TEEN mom Kailyn Lowry is a reality TV star known for her feisty personality and determination to defy the odds stacked against her.

She rose to international fame after falling pregnant at 17 and starring on MTV's 16 And Pregnant, and Teen Mom 2.

What is Kailyn Lowry’s net worth?

Kailyn Lowry was born on March 14, 1992, in Nazareth, Pennsylvania, and fell pregnant with her first child in 2009.

The small-town girl was cast on MTV's hit reality TV show 16 and Pregnant and gave birth to her son Isaac Elliot Rivera in January 2010.

She was later selected for a follow-up series, Teen Mom 2.

Now an author of four books, including New York Times bestseller Pride Over Pity and children's book Love Is Bubblegum, Kailyn only seems to be adding more to her load.

She co-hosts podcasts Coffee Convos with another influencer, Lindsie Chrisley, and Baby Mamas No Drama with MTV star Vee Rivera - and hosts her own podcast, Barely Famous.

As of October 2023, she has more than 4.5 million followers on Instagram.

Her book publications, merchandise-selling ventures, and podcast shows have earned her prominence and financial success.

She now has an estimated net worth of $1 million, reports therichest.com.

When did Kailyn Lowry leave Teen Mom?

The reality TV alum announced she was "done" with Teen Mom in April 2022 after 11 years, telling Perez Hilton on her Barely Famous podcast she thought the show had "run its course".

Speaking about whether she would return for future seasons, Lowry said: "I don't think so. Right now, the way I look at it, I just don't want to."

Rumors swirled in the aftermath of her announcement that she was difficult to film with.

How many children does Kailyn Lowry have?

Lowry has a total of seven children.

In November 2023, it was revealed by the U.S. Sun that she had given birth to twins, a boy and a girl, with boyfriend Elijah Scott.

On November 10, Kailyn confirmed the sex of her twins in a gender reveal video posted to her social media pages.

Kailyn first confirmed she was pregnant with twins on October 27 following months of speculation.

Lowry went to Thailand in early 2023 and revealed on her podcast Barely Famous: "I must have got pregnant right before I left and I had no idea.

"So when I got there, I was eating everything. My face was flushed, but I didn't think anything of it because I was like, there's no way. There's no way I am.

"Well, to be fair, I mean, this is my... sixth pregnancy, like six and seven for me.

"And I've gotten pregnant when I'm not actually... like I've tracked my ovulation and I've gotten pregnant on days that were not my ovulation window."

The news came about a month after Lowry was spotted outside her Delaware podcast studio with what appeared to be a baby bump.

The MTV star is a mother to son, Isaac, 13, with her ex, Jo Rivera; son, Lincoln, nine, with her ex-husband, Javi Marroquin; sons, Lux, six; and Creed, three, with her ex, Chris Lopez; and a son, Rio, 11 months, with her boyfriend, Elijah Scott.

She found love with her ex-husband Marroquin while filming Teen Mom 2's fifth season and gave birth to their son Lincoln in November 2013.

In April 2015, the pair revealed they had suffered a miscarriage at 25 weeks. They ended their marriage a few months later.

Lowry welcomed a son with her ex Lopez in August 2017, and a second baby boy in 2020.
