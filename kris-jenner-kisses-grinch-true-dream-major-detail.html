KRIS Jenner has gotten into the holiday spirit, cozying up to the Grinch in a Sunday Instagram post that had fans saying, ho-ho-hold on.

The momager treated True Thompson, 5, Khloe Kardashian, 39, and Dream Kardashian, 7, to a festive musical outing.

The Instagram snaps followed their experience at the live show of "How the Grinch Stole Christmas."

The group seized the opportunity for a photo op against the show's backdrop, with Kris, 68, giving the Grinch a light peck on the cheek.

In that snap, Rob and Blac Chyna's daughter Dream sported a smile alongside Khloe's daughter True with Tristan Thomspon- and a pal.

In the second, Khloe joined in, donning a baggy black jacket and a smile.
