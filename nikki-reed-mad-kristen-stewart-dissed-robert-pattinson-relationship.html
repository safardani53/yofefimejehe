In case you don’t remember, Nikki Reed, 28, dated Robert Pattinson, 30, BEFORE Kristen Stewart, 26, kinda, sorta’ stole him away. It caused a great deal of tension on the Twilight set, but now that Nikki is happily married to Ian Somerhalder, she couldn’t care less about the petty drama. “It took a long time for Nikki to forgive Kristen for meddling in her relationship with Rob,” tells a source EXCLUSIVELY to HollywoodLife.com. “But as time went on, she learned how to get over it. Once she married Paul [McDonald], she completely forgot about the whole thing, and now she’s with Ian who’s considered her soulmate. Rob is just a blurb in her life story, so she isn’t really paying or giving much attention to Kristen’s diss.”
