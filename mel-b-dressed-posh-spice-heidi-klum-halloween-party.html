Mel B mocks Victoria Beckham's refusal to join Spice Girls tour by dressing as her for Halloween

 Posted on October 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  337 words  |  Larita Shotwell

MEL B has ramped up her war with Victoria Beckham by dressing as her former Spice Girl bandmate for Halloween.

Scary Spice cheekily wore a Posh Spice face mask and little black dress to Heidi Klum’s costume party.

She also carried a sign announcing: “No I am not going on tour” — a reference to Victoria’s U-turn on taking part in the band’s reunion.

Mel, 43, went to the New York bash with pal Gary Madatyan, who pretended to be Victoria’s husband David in an LA Galaxy kit. Their jokey get-ups come just weeks after The Sun on Sunday revealed that Mel called Victoria, 44, a “b***h” for refusing to take part in the Spice Girls’ revival.

Mel is desperate for all five members to be part of the comeback, previously saying she would “hate it” if Posh didn’t feature.

A Spice source revealed: “Mel is having a laugh right now and being very naughty.

Heidi Klum transforms into Princess Fiona for her annual Halloween party

“She’s hoping Victoria sees the funny side.

“They’ve known each other for so long and been through so much together — and Mel just can’t resist a cheeky wind-up.” The Sun has led revelations about the highly anticipated reunion, with more major developments expected within days.

Posh had said she’d take part then pulled out — but still championed the band’s brand in a Vogue shoot.

Mel B, Mel C, Emma Bunton and Geri Horner will continue, with a tour planned for next year.
