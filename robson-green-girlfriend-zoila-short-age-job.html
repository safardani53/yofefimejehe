ROBSON Green shot to fame as a young heartthrob in the 90s, when he burst onto the scene in Soldier, Soldier.

The Unchained Melody star continues to wow viewers on ITV's Grantchester, but when he's not busy working he enjoys a cosy home life with girlfriend Zoila Short.

Who is Robson Green's girlfriend Zoila Short?

Zoila Short is the girlfriend of Robson Green and a former Sunday School teacher.

She was previously married to the Reverend Geoffrey Short.

Her ex-husband Geoffrey previously opened up about the "shock" moment when she ended their relationship.

Speaking to the The Mirror he said: "We had a great life together. People thought she was the ideal vicar's wife, and she was.

"She said they got talking in the gym and she fell in love. She left ­immediately."

When did Robson Green meet Zoila?

The pair first started dating in April 2016 after she met him at a gym in the suburbs of Newcastle.

But the couple were first snapped together on the set of ITV's Grantchester in August 2018.

They now live together at his home in Northumberland.

However, it doesn't look like they will be getting married anytime soon.

Speaking to The Times he said:"No, I’ve done enough of that. We’re very happy together."

Who else has Robson Green dated?

Robson's first wife was Alison Ogilvie who he was married to from 1991 to 1999.
He then married Vanya Seager in 2001 but the couple divorced in 2011.

Their son, Taylor-Seager Green was born in 2000.

In 2013 he had a brief relationship with flight attendant Stephanie Short.
