Carrie Fisher: John Travolta Gay Rumors True

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  294 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Carrie Fisher claims John Travolta is gay…

Actress Carrie Fisher had no issue discussing the alleged sexual preferences of actor John Travolta in a recent interview with the Advocate. Plagued by “gay” rumors since the 1980’s, John isn’t catching a break from Carrie — who claims: “Everyone knows John Travolta is gay.”

Travolta has been married to actress Kelly Preston since 1991 and they are the parents of three children: Ella Bleu, 10, newborn baby Benjamin and son Jett, 16, who died two years ago following a seizure.

Carrie was asked if Travolta’s legal team should go after Gawker website, demanding that they recant suggestions that he provided sexual favors to other men.

Her response: “Wow! I mean, my feeling about John has always been that we know and we don’t care. Look, I’m sorry that he’s uncomfortable with it, and that’s all I can say. It only draws more attention to it when you make that kind of legal fuss. Just leave it be.”

This isn’t the first time that Fisher has discussed this issue. In 2009 for a story about 10 Things gay men should know about women – she said: “We don’t really care that John Travolta is gay.”

The rumors first surfaced years ago when gay porn star Paul Barresi stated that he and John had an affair back in the 1980’s. Others have come forward with the same claims. Apparently there is a new book about to publish that has Travolta lawyers scrambling because the author of the book claims to have evidence of Travolta’s “secret gay life.”

Do you think Carrie has any business talking about the sexual preferences of a happily married man with children? We don’t.
