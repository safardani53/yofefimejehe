I'm a redheaded gym girl - I'm able to change my look by tossing hair but men say there's one reason

 Posted on September 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  276 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A REDHEADED gym girl has said she can change her whole look with a simple toss of her head - but men say there's one reason they never notice.

Model and OnlyFans star Cristina Carmella took to TikTok in a gray bralette to show off the trick with her luscious waves.

The ginger started with her hair combed neatly and falling behind her, showing off her ears, shoulders, collarbone, and bralette straps.

Cristina began transforming her gym look by bringing her long hair over her shoulders so it fell over her front while remaining well-combed.

She then ruffled the top of her hair to frame her face with graduated layers - changing her look from "clean girl" to sultry siren.

The shape-shifting beauty cited the the trick as a demonstration of "hair theory," the belief held by many TikTok users that changing your hairstyle can change the way you look.

Cristina said: "Felt obligated to do this as a ginger."

But men on TikTok proved uninterested in the finer points of "hair theory" - with many saying there's one reason they didn't notice.
