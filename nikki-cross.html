Who is Nikki Cross:

Nikki Cross is a famous Wrestler. She was born on April 21, 1989 and her birthplace is Glasgow, Scotland. Nikki is also well known as, Scottish professional wrestler who is widely known for her tenure on WWE’s NXT promotion. In October 2016, she debuted as a member of the heel stable SAnitY.

Nikki is originated from Scotland. She was a member of the stable SAnitY for WWE NXT alongside Sawyer Fulton, Eric Young, and Alexander Wolf.
