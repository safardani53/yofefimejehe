Khloe Kardashian jokes she's 'such a fatty' after star sparks concern with very thin thighs and dras

 Posted on August 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  608 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

KHLOE Kardashian has caused some concern after she joked that she's "such a fatty" amid existing fear among fans about her weight loss.

The star made the comment on her Instagram story as she showed off a gift from sister Kourtney Kardashian in celebration of the Lemme launch.

On Tuesday, Khloe, 38, posted a video of herself showing off a gumball machine she was sent by Kourtney, 43.

The branded machine appeared to be filled with gumballs but dispensed vitamins when the Good American founder turned the dial.

She held an empty plastic ball in her hand as she joked: "I'm such a fatty that I ate it all."

Khloe's comment comes amid existing concern from her followers about her weight, which has shifted drastically in recent months.

The mom-of-two shared a new photo alongside mom Kris Jenner, 66, that shocked her followers.

In the photo, taken during Milan fashion week, Khloe donned a '90s catsuit with diamond detail.

Fans were stunned at how skinny she looked in the snaps and made it known in the comments.

Her thighs, in particular, looked frighteningly slim to some.
