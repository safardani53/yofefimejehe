Disney wedding dresses now exist and were absolutely enchanted

 Posted on June 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  175 words  |  Larita Shotwell

IF you're a Disney super fan, you might want to sit down for this one.

A company in Japan has created a collection of Disney-themed wedding dresses so you can actually look like a princess on your big day.

From Belle to Snow White and Cinderella - take your pick, because Kyoto-based company Kuraudia Co has them all.

There's even Ariel from The Little Mermaid as well as Rapunzel.

In fact, the company has 14 princess styles up for grabs.

And the groom needn't feel left out either, as they even offer a selection of suits inspired by Disney princes.

The gowns are available to rent - but sadly, are currently only available for brides-to-be in Japan (or those getting hitched there).

While the fee for a princess-inspired gown is around £2,800, a groom in Japan could get his hands on a Disney tuxedo for just under £700.

Talk about a fairy-tale wedding - which style is your favourite?
