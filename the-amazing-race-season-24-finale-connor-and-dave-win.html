Dave & Connor O’Leary Win ‘The Amazing Race’ Season 24

Congrats are in order for Dave and Connor O’Leary! They are officially the big winners of The Amazing Race!

The father-son duo from Salt Lake City is the first parent-child team to ever win, and are also the oldest and the youngest winners to ever nab the big title.

The big season ender on May 18 ended with Dave, 59, and Connor, 22, hitting the finish line, prevailing over the other finalists Brendan & Rachel and Caroline & Jen. After being blasted around the world and spending last week’s episode in London, the top three teams were taken to Las Vegas for their final competition, which revolved around magician David Copperfield and sent the players on a search around the city.

Let’s just say that magic happened for Connor and Dave, as they uncovered the clues and raced to the mat first!

Connor announced the exciting news on his Twitter right when the show ended.

My old man and I pulled it off! We just won the @AmazingRace_CBS! #AmazingRace

— Connor O’Leary (@Coleary00) May 19, 2014
