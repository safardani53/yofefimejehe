Karolina Kowalkiewicz | Professional Career

Before gaining her fame and wealth with the UFC, Karolina fought for several fighting promotions.

Firstly, she started her career in Poland, where she amassed an undefeated record of seven wins and no losses.

Added to that, Karolina also won her first career belt with the KSW organization as their Flyweight Champion.

As a result, she caught the eye of a women-only fighting organization, Invicta FC, who signed her in 2014.

Subsequently, staying true to her form, Karolina dispatched Mizuki Inoue to mark her US debut with a victory. After that, she returned to Poland and fought one more time before signing for UFC in 2015.

UFC

On her debut, Kowalkiewicz defeated Randa Markos via a unanimous decision to take her professional record to eight wins and zero losses.

After that, the fighter defeated two more opponents in the UFC before getting her title shot against then Strawweight Champion Joanna Jedzejczyk.

Unfortunately, Karolina lost to her fellow countryman Joanna via a unanimous decision. After that, Kowalkiewicz fought in six matches, recording an alarming four losses and only two wins. However, her worst was yet to come.
