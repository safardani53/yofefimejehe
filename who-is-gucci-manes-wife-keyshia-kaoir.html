KEYSHIA Ka'oir has built a successful career.

Although she's married to a highly famous rapper, the 37-year-old has created a name for herself.

Who is Gucci Mane's wife Keyshia Ka'oir?

Keyshia Ka'oir was born Keyshia Watson on January 6, 1985, in Kingston, Jamaica.

She married Gucci Mane in 2017.

The pair share a young son named Ice Davis and are expecting their second child together.

Keyshia announced the news on Instagram in a post from September 9, 2022.

In addition to being a mother, Keyshia is a businesswoman, model, and social media influencer.

Keyshia and her husband also starred in a ten-episode TV series about their nuptials titled Gucci Mane and Keyshia Ka'Oir: The Mane Event.

What is Ka'oir Fitness?

Keyshia is the CEO of Ka'oir Fitness, a fitness company that first launched in 2013.

The Ka'oir Waist Eraser was the brand's first product.

On the company's website, Keyshia states: "I continued to expand KA’OIR Fitness. Due to the amazing response from my customers sending before and after pictures, it motivated me to continue with more effective products."

She continues: "I then launched a Body Sweat which I like to refer to as a “Body Eraser”! The Body Sweat makes you sweat so much more, burning more calories & speeding up your metabolism, which allows you to lose weight all over."

Keyshia then discusses her other products, such as Thigh Erasers and a Body Burner Slimming Cream, before stating she has a "passion for fitness."

In addition to fitness, Keyshia is also involved in the makeup and hair industries.

She is the CEO of Ka'oir Cosmetics and Ka'oir Hair.

Does Keyshia Ka'oir post about her relationship on social media?

On November 13, 2022, she posted an image with her spouse, along with the caption: "The Mr & Mrs."

She also added a red heart emoji.
