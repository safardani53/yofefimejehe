Gwen Stefani's fans divided over wild new Voice outfit some fans call 'stunning' while others think

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  618 words  |  Martina Birk

GWEN Stefani has her fans split after she shared snaps of herself in an eccentric ensemble on social media.

The 54-year-old Voice coach had people debating in her Instagram comment section whether her outfit was a hit or miss.

Gwen uploaded a few images of the ensemble she was going to wear on tonight's episode in a carousel post on the platform.

The Hollaback Girl singer donned a cropped white button-down shirt and pencil skirt with big denim-colored circles on it.

Around Gwen's neck was a denim blue tie with white polka dots.

The TV personality donned an oversized jacket that matched her necktie, which she left open.

Under the award-winning songstress' clothes were black fishnets that covered her legs, and the area of skin left exposed by the midriff.

Gwen pulled the look together with blue and white shoes.

"And we r back 2night !!! #thevoice," she captioned her post.

Gwen had many fans who complimented her fashion choice for Monday's episode.

FAN REACTIONS

"Gwen can wear a trash bag and make it look good," one fan shared.

Another added: "This outfit is so rad. Lovvvve it."

A third person raved: "Gorgeous inside and out!!"

A fourth fan agreed: "Everything you wear looks great on you."

A fifth person asserted: "One of my favorite outfits this season."

In comparison to how many liked her outfit, Gwen had only a few haters.

However, there were critics who shaded her look.

"Miss... You have a chalk stain on your jacket.. No2...no3..4..5," a fan slated.

Another slammed: "Ugly ugly outfit."

RECENT BACKLASH

Critics are often outspoken in their dislike of the former No Doubt frontwoman.

During Monday's all-new episode of The Voice, viewers watched as judges were forced to pit two of their team members against one another.

The duos were tasked with singing the same song together, with judges then picking one singer as the winner of the challenge.

Many judges struggled with the decision but some more than others.

Gwen, in particular, appeared to have a hard time choosing who she wanted to dub victorious - and it frustrated some The Voice viewers.

One critic complained on X, formerly Twitter: "Sick of Gwen never making a choice, didn't miss her at all."

The award-winning musician returned to the show in Season 24 after taking some time away.

While her fans are thrilled to have her back on the stage, others would rather she stayed away.

Gwen's return comes after her husband Blake Shelton, 47, stepped back from hosting The Voice.

MOVING ON?

She is often mocked by viewers, whether it's for her behavior or her outfits.

Amid the backlash, Gwen announced some exciting career news away from the show.

The Hollywood Walk of Fame titleholder revealed on Monday that she will be headlining Cali Vibes Fest in Long Beach, California.

She shared the news in an Instagram post.

Gwen revealed the festival's animated poster, which listed all the stars that will perform.

Shaggy, 55, Ice Cube, 54, and Wiz Khalifa, 36, are just a few of the big names chosen to take the stage during the three-day festival.

Gwen's caption read: "SOCAL!

"Can't wait to sing with you Saturday, Feb 17th at @calivibesfest!"

Her single Let Me Reintroduce Myself was playing in the background.

The Cali Vibes Instagram account replied in the comments with: "Welcome to Vibes! We're all so excited to have you!"

Her fans sent congratulatory messages in the comments, with one writing: "Got my ticket! I even already bought my outfit! Hahaha! Can't wait! See you in February!"
