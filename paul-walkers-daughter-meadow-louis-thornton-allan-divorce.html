MEADOW Walker and Louis Thornton-Allan have announced they are getting a divorce.

The two - who got married in 2021 - shared the news on social media on Wednesday.

The daughter, 25, of the late Hollywood actor Paul Walker shared the news on Instagram in a joint statement.

"After three wonderful years of marriage, we have come to the agreement to amicably separate," the post read.

"This is truly a united decision and we sincerely hope that everyone can respect our wishes for privacy. We maintain and mutual love and respect for one another, and will continue to support each other."
