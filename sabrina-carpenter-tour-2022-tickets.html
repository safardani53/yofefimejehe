SABRINA Carpenter announced that she plans on bringing her music live to a variety of cities.

The tour is in promotion of Sabrina's fifth studio album, Emails I Can't Send.

When is Sabrina Carpenter's Emails I Can't Send tour?

Sabrina Carpenter's 2022 tour kicked off in Atlanta, Georgia on Thursday, September 29.

Her fifth album, Emails I Can't Send, reached the 23rd spot on Billboard's Top 200.

Sabrina's song, Skinny Dipping, reached over 18million listens on Spotify as of August 16, with Fast Times reaching 17.1million listens and Because I Liked a Boy reaching 12.8million listens.

The title track on the album reached 5.8million listens at the same timeframe and the singer spoke about her inspiration for the title track on the album.

Sabrina told Vogue: “When I wrote the actual title track, I was [using] one of the emails that I had written to myself, and I just said out loud: ‘That’s the name of the album!’

"Then every song kind of came from that place. Every song came from those emails or messages or whatever my way of coping was at the time."

The Tall Girl actress added: "I think it captures a really important time in my life. I would hope that if someone had never listened to my music before, and they listened to this album, they would leave it feeling like they know me better as a person.

"I was worried for a second that it wasn’t the fully confident pop record that fans who have been following me for a long time might be coming to my music for.

"Taking personal situations in my life and being able to turn them into art was always a way of healing myself, and also understanding those situations a bit better."

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for Carpenter's tour went on pre-sale at 10am on Tuesday, August 16, and can be purchased on Carpenter's official website.

A few days later, on Friday, August 19, general tickets went on sale at 10am.
