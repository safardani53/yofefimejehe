KHLOE Kardashian has shown off her polished nails in a new social media post.

The Hulu star shared the clip on her Instagram Stories on Tuesday.

Khloe, 39, spent a part of her busy day at a nail salon in Los Angeles, California.

In the short clip, the Good American co-founder flaunted her left hand to give her 311million social media followers a glimpse at her razor-sharp tips.

While speaking in the video, she revealed she had on those nails for two weeks - which was a light pink set that had a brown snakeskin pattern on the ends.

In a small font, she tagged the artist who painted her nails, Chaun Legend.

IS TRUE OKAY?
