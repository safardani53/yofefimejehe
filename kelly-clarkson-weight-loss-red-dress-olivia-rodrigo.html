One clip showed the television star alongside singer Olivia Rodrigo, 20, as they announced that they were ready to "surprise some fans."

Kelly had her usually wavy hair straightened, with the top section clipped back.

She wore bright red lipstick, which perfectly matched her bold, festive dress.

SECRET WEAPON?

While Kelly hasn't credited any special tools for her weight loss, a physician who specializes in nutritional medicine, told The U.S. Sun that she might be using Ozempic to secure her new figure.

Dr. Fred Pescatore comparing photos from March 2023 of Kelly at the iHeartRadio Music Awards with photos from November 2023 of the star at a SiriusXM event, and concluded that she's lost around 50 pounds.

While the talk show host has not admitted to using Ozempic or any similar weight loss drugs, Dr. Pescatore, who has not treated nor has any ties to Kelly, suggests use of it.

"It is possible she is on a diet, but all things point to Ozempic or Mounjaro," he claimed, referencing the before and after photos.

When asked if it could be something she's ashamed of admitting publicly, Dr. Pescatore said that sometimes celebrities "do extreme things and don't want the world to know."

He added: "In her case, she is a talk show host and has to be relatable.

"Taking a drug that is $1,000 per month isn't very relatable to the women who watch her show."

DROPPING SIZES

Kelly joined The Voice as a judge a year after the birth of her son, and later admitted to Glamour UK that she was at her heaviest point then.

Fans of the singer were stunned when she debuted a smaller figure in 2018, and Kelly claimed she changed her diet to foods organic foods.

Around that time Kelly said: “It’s when I’m fat that I’m happy. People think, ‘Oh, there’s something wrong with her. She’s putting on weight,'” she told Redbook.

“I’m like, ‘Oh, no! I’m sorry, but that represents happiness in my emotional world. For me, when I’m skinny is usually when I’m not doing well.”

Despite previously suggesting she was happier when larger, Kelly recently gushed about losing weight while speaking to Jenna Bush Hager on Today.
