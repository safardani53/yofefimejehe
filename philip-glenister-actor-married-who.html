Who is actor Philip Glenister and is he married?

 Posted on July 1, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  451 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

OVER the years Philip Glenister has cemented himself as one of Britain's leading actors.

The talented star's career has spanned over three decades, and includes a very iconic noughties crime drama.

Who is Philip Glenister?

Philip Glenister was born on February 10, 1963 in Harrow, Middlesex, and grew up in Hatch End.

He is the son of director John Glenister, and the younger brother of fellow actor Robert Glenister.

Up until the age of 23 he worked as a film publicist but developed an interest in acting after watching his brother perform in a student play about the Sex Pistols.

With the encouragement of his then-sister-in-law Amanda Redman, he pursued acting and attended the Central School of Speech and Drama.

What has Philip Glenister starred in?

In the early 1990s, Philip appeared in various TV series including Minder, The Ruth Rendell Mysteries, Heartbeat, The Chief, Dressing for Breakfast and Silent Witness.

In 1997, he appeared in Sharpe's Justice as Richard Sharpe's half-brother Matt Truman.

However, in the mid-noughties he became a household name when he starred in the smash hit crime drama Life On Mars.

He played chauvinist cop, DCI Gene Hunt, who was was famed for his catchphrase, "Fire up the Quattro."

Unlike co-star John Simm , he reprised the role in spin-off show Ashes To Ashes, and after that ended in 2010, has been an in demand actor.

In 2011, Phillip reunited with John in the Sky TV mini-series Mad Dogs about a group of old friends whose holiday in Majorca takes an unexpected turn.

His other big acting roles include taking on the lead role in the ITV period drama Belgravia, which aired in the UK and America.

Philip also starred as Reverend Anderson on American horror drama Outcast, as well as in the BBC drama Cranford.

In 2023 the talented actor took the lead in BBC One's true crime drama the Steeltown Murders.

Away from acting, Philip also presented the true crime series, What the Killer Did Next.

Is Philip Glenister married?

Phillip is married to actress Beth Goddard, who is known for her role as Suze Littlewood in the BBC comedy Gimme Gimme Gimme.

The pair met at a birthday party for actor Jamie Glover in 1997, and were wed in 2006.

The couple have two daughters, Millie and Charlotte, born in 2002 and 2005 respectively.
