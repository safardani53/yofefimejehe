Who is Marian 'GG' on Doubling Down With the Derricos?

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  258 words  |  Martina Birk

THE Derrico family is made up of husband, Deon, wife, Karen, and their 14 children.

Deon's mother, Marian, who the family calls 'GG,' is also a familiar face that is seen in the Derrico household on TLC's Doubling Down with the Derricos.

Who is Marian 'GG' on Doubling Down With the Derricos?

GG is the children's paternal grandmother and is often seen helping Karen and Deon take care of the kids.

While not much information is available on the lovable grandma, as of September 2020, GG was 70 years old.

GG has always been the supportive grandmother, although when Deon and Karen announced that they were expecting their 15th child, she was not shy to voice her concerns.

However, it was later revealed that Karen suffered another miscarriage.

How many kids does Marian 'GG' Derrico have?

GG welcomed two children over the course of her life - Christopher Turner and Deon Derrico. Christoper, unfortunately, died in 2020.

She is also the grandmother to 14 children.

Those grandchildren include:

  • Darian, 17
  • Derrick, 12
  • Twins Dallas and Denver, 11
  • Quintuplets, Deonee, Daician, Daiten, Deniko and Dariz, 9
  • Twins Diez and Dior, 5
  • Triplets Dawsyn, De'Aren, and Dyver, 3
