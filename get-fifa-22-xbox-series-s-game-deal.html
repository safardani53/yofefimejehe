Get FIFA 22 and a brand new Xbox Series S from just 85 at GAME

 Posted on August 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  440 words  |  Aldo Pusey

GAMERS looking for a steal on a next-gen console are in luck with a fantastic deal from GAME.

Running for a limited time only, you can trade in your existing console to get an Xbox Series S and FIFA 22 from just £84.99.

The trade-in value is dependant on the console you're turning in, and if you want to partake, you'll need a GAME Reward account.

The deal is available between Friday, October 1 and Sunday, October 10.

The Xbox Series S is Microsoft's digital-only console, and offers a more compact form factor than the Xbox Series X.

It's also less powerful, and ergo less expensive; you won't get 4k graphics and the load times are a little slower.

You can read up on the main differences between the Xbox Series S and Xbox Series X if you need to swot up.

But if you're not fussed about 4k, or have younger gamers in mind, the Xbox Series S is the perfect next-gen console for you.

Plus the cheaper price tag of £249 / $299 makes it even more appealing than its £449 / $499 counterpart.

And with this deal, it's even cheaper. Here's what you'll end up paying based on the console you trade in (via VGC):
